# Data Sources — European Union

## EU Official Sources
This project uses only official European Union and EU-retained law publishers.
No scraping, no secondary aggregators, no unofficial mirrors.

EUR-Lex (European Union)
- Publisher: Publications Office of the European Union
- Content Type: EU Regulations, Directives, Decisions, Implementing and Delegated Acts
- Access Method: Official EUR-Lex APIs, XML, RDF, CSV, and HTML endpoints
- Base URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu
- License: EU Open Data Directive / EUR-Lex reuse policy
- Notes:
  -This is the authoritative source of European Union law.
  -Only officially published metadata, summaries, and document links are processed.
  -Full legislative texts are linked, not redistributed.

The National Archives (UK) — Retained & Referenced EU Law
- Publisher: The National Archives (UK)
- Content Type:
- Retained EU law
- EU Regulations and Directives as incorporated into UK law
- EU instruments referenced for historical or comparative purposes
- Access Method: Official XML, RDF, CSV, and HTML endpoints
- Base URL: https://www.legislation.gov.uk
- License: Open Government Licence v3.0
- Notes:
  - This source is used only where EU legislation is:
  - Retained in UK law

Referenced for continuity, amendment history, or cross-jurisdictional comparison
No UK-only interpretation is inferred for EU-only instruments.

Legal & Compliance Statement
- No scraping or automated extraction of restricted content is performed.
- All data is obtained from official government sources.
- Content is used in accordance with the stated licenses.
- Where full documents are not available via APIs, only metadata and summaries are included.

Only official summaries, abstracts, and metadata are used. Full legal texts, standards documents, and PDFs are not republished.

All underlying source documents remain the property of their respective issuing authorities.
